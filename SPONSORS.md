# Sponsor 8gent

8gent Jr is a free communication app for non-verbal children. It was built by a single father for his autistic son Nick, who deserves a voice -- not a subscription paywall.

**8gent Jr will be free forever.** No ads. No tracking. No selling data. GDPR-compliant by design, because children's privacy is a right, not a feature.

This project is sustained entirely by community sponsors.

---

## What Sponsorship Supports

- **Server infrastructure** -- Convex (real-time backend), Vercel (hosting)
- **Text-to-speech services** -- ElevenLabs for natural-sounding child voices
- **Development time** -- A solo developer building across mobile, web, and backend
- **Accessibility testing** -- Real-world testing with speech therapists and families

Every dollar goes directly to keeping the lights on and shipping features that help kids communicate.

## Our Commitments

- **Open source** -- the code is public, the mission is transparent

- **No ads, ever** -- children are not a monetization opportunity

## The Story

Nick is non-verbal. Before 8gent Jr, communication meant pointing, guessing, and frustration on both sides. AAC (Augmentative and Alternative Communication) apps exist, but they cost hundreds of dollars, lock families into subscriptions, and treat accessibility as a premium feature.

That is wrong.

8gent Jr exists because every child deserves to say what they are thinking, regardless of what their parents can afford.
