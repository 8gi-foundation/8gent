## 3. Project Description

### The Problem

Approximately 1 in 100 children in Ireland are autistic. Many are non-verbal or have limited speech. These children rely on AAC (Augmentative and Alternative Communication) to express basic needs, feelings, and thoughts.

The current options are failing families:

- **Dedicated AAC devices** cost EUR 3,000--8,000 per unit. Most families wait 12--24 months for HSE approval and funding.
- **Existing AAC apps** (Proloquo2Go, TD Snap) cost EUR 250--400 and run only on iPad. They use static symbol libraries with no personalisation.
- **HSE-provided services** face chronic understaffing. Children's Disability Network Teams have multi-year waiting lists. Budget 2026 added 150 therapists, but demand far outstrips supply.

My son Nick is 8 years old, autistic, and non-verbal. He has used AAC devices since he was 3. I built 8gent Jr because every existing tool felt like it was designed in 2010.

### What 8gent Jr Does

8gent Jr is a web-based AAC application. Children tap picture cards to build sentences that are spoken aloud via text-to-speech. It runs on any device with a browser -- no special hardware required.

What makes it different from existing solutions:

- **AI-powered personalisation.** The app learns which cards a child uses most frequently and surfaces them first, reducing the number of taps to communicate. A child who always asks for "juice" at 3pm will see that card promoted automatically.
- **GLP-stage awareness.** Many autistic children acquire language through Gestalt Language Processing -- whole phrases before individual words. 8gent Jr supports this by offering scripted phrases alongside single-word cards, something no commercial AAC app currently does.
- **Runs on any device.** Web-based. Works on a EUR 200 Android tablet, a school Chromebook, or an iPhone. No EUR 5,000 dedicated device needed.
- **Free forever for families.** The core AAC functionality will never be behind a paywall. This is a commitment, not a marketing strategy.
- **GDPR-first architecture.** A full Data Protection Impact Assessment has been completed. Parental consent gates are enforced before any child data is processed. Data minimisation is built into the schema, not bolted on.

The app is live in early beta at 8gentjr.com and is being used daily by my son.

## 6. Feasibility Study Objectives

This grant would fund a structured 6-month feasibility study to answer four questions:

### 6.1 Clinical Validation
- **Question:** Does AI-powered card personalisation measurably reduce time-to-communicate for non-verbal children?
- **Method:** Recruit 8--12 families through SLT networks. Conduct supervised user testing sessions. Measure taps-to-communicate, session duration, and vocabulary growth over 8 weeks.
- **Success metric:** 20%+ reduction in average taps-to-communicate vs. static card layouts.

### 6.2 Professional Endorsement
- **Question:** Do Speech and Language Therapists see clinical value in GLP-stage-aware AAC?
- **Method:** Engage 3--5 SLTs for paid consultancy. Structured interviews and product review sessions. Gather written feedback on clinical applicability.
- **Success metric:** Formal written endorsement from at least 2 practising SLTs.

### 6.3 Regulatory and Legal Compliance
- **Question:** Is the product compliant with GDPR, the EU AI Act, and medical device regulations (if applicable)?
- **Method:** Commission a legal review of the DPIA. Obtain opinion on whether the AI personalisation feature triggers medical device classification under MDR 2017/745. Review EU AI Act obligations for high-risk education AI.
- **Success metric:** Written legal opinion confirming regulatory pathway.

### 6.4 Business Model Validation
- **Question:** Is there a sustainable revenue model that keeps the product free for families?
- **Method:** Interview 5--10 institutional buyers (schools, HSE procurement, CDNT managers). Test pricing for institutional licences and therapist analytics dashboards.
- **Success metric:** Letters of interest or expressions of intent from at least 2 institutional buyers.
